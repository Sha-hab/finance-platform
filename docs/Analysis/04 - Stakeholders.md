# 04 - Stakeholders

## Purpose

This document identifies the primary stakeholders of Finance Platform and explains how each is expected to interact with the system. Understanding the intended users ensures the platform is designed to solve real problems while remaining intuitive and accessible.

---

# Introduction

Finance Platform is designed to support a range of users with different levels of financial experience and varying amounts of long-term wealth. Although their financial situations differ, each stakeholder shares a common objective: to understand where their wealth is held before making decisions about how to grow or manage it.

The following stakeholder profiles represent the primary users considered throughout the design and development of the platform.

---

# Stakeholder 1 - Project Owner

## Stakeholder Profile

University student building long-term wealth.

## Current Financial Situation

- Multiple savings accounts.
- Investments.
- Physical assets such as gold.
- Plans to acquire additional long-term assets in the future.

## Why are they interested in Finance Platform?

The project owner wants a single location to view all long-term wealth rather than checking multiple banking and investment platforms. They also want to understand how their wealth changes over time and build a platform that can continue growing alongside their financial journey.

## How will they use Finance Platform?

- Record savings.
- Record investments.
- Record physical assets.
- Monitor total net worth.
- Track historical changes.
- Review the performance of individual assets.

## Goals

- Understand total net worth.
- Know where wealth is held.
- Track historical performance.
- Build a long-term financial record.

## Success Criteria

The platform provides a complete, accurate and easy-to-understand overview of all long-term wealth from a single dashboard.

---

# Stakeholder 2 - Student Saver

## Stakeholder Profile

University student focused on building savings before entering the investment market.

## Current Financial Situation

- Two savings accounts.
- Small emergency fund.
- No investments.

## Why are they interested in Finance Platform?

They want to develop good financial habits by understanding how much they have saved and monitoring their progress towards future financial goals.

## How will they use Finance Platform?

- Record savings accounts.
- Update balances periodically.
- View total savings.
- Monitor net worth growth over time.

## Goals

- Build consistent saving habits.
- Understand financial progress.
- Prepare for future investing.

## Success Criteria

The platform makes it simple to understand how savings are growing and provides clear historical insight into financial progress.

---

# Stakeholder 3 - Student Investor

## Stakeholder Profile

University student beginning to build an investment portfolio.

## Current Financial Situation

- Savings account.
- Stocks & Shares ISA.
- Small portfolio of ETFs and individual shares.

## Why are they interested in Finance Platform?

They want to understand how their savings and investments contribute to their overall wealth without switching between multiple applications.

## How will they use Finance Platform?

- Record savings.
- Record investments.
- Update portfolio values.
- Track overall net worth.
- Monitor historical performance.

## Goals

- Combine savings and investments in one place.
- Understand investment growth.
- Monitor overall financial progress.

## Success Criteria

The platform successfully combines savings and investments into a single, organised view that clearly shows changes in overall wealth.

---

# Common Requirements

Although each stakeholder has different financial circumstances, they all require the platform to:

- Provide an accurate overview of long-term wealth.
- Clearly display where wealth is held.
- Track historical changes in net worth.
- Present financial information in a simple and accessible manner.
- Remain easy to use regardless of financial experience.

---

## Summary

### Key Points

- Finance Platform is designed for users with varying levels of financial experience.
- Each stakeholder has different financial circumstances but shares similar objectives.
- The platform prioritises understanding long-term wealth before financial optimisation.
- Stakeholder requirements guide the design of the Beta release.
