# 01 - Problem Analysis

## Purpose

This document defines the problem that Finance Platform aims to solve and explains why a new solution is required.

---

# Introduction

As individuals accumulate savings, investments, property and other long-term assets, their wealth often becomes spread across multiple institutions and platforms. While each provider offers tools to manage its own services, very few provide a complete overview of an individual's financial position.

---

# The Problem

Long-term wealth is fragmented.

Users frequently store money and assets across multiple locations, making it difficult to understand:

- Their current net worth.
- Where their wealth is held.
- How individual assets perform.
- How their overall wealth changes over time.

Obtaining this information often requires manually combining data from multiple applications or spreadsheets.

---

# Existing Solutions

Most existing financial applications specialise in a single area.

Examples include:

- Banking applications
- Investment platforms
- Property services
- Budgeting applications
- Spreadsheets

Although these solutions perform their intended purpose effectively, none provide a complete overview of long-term wealth within a single platform.

---

# Opportunity

Finance Platform is built upon one guiding principle:

> **Before people can start making money, they need to know where their money is.**

Rather than focusing on investment advice or automation, the project aims to help users understand their financial position by bringing together all long-term assets and liabilities within one application.

---

# Problem Statement

People often have long-term wealth distributed across multiple locations, making it difficult to understand their complete financial position.

Finance Platform addresses this problem by providing a centralised platform that allows users to monitor their net worth, organise their wealth and track historical changes over time.

---

## Summary

### Key Points

- Long-term wealth is commonly fragmented.
- Existing solutions focus on individual financial products.
- Understanding wealth should come before optimisation.
- Finance Platform provides a centralised overview of long-term wealth.
