# 02 - Project Vision

## Purpose

This document defines the long-term direction of Finance Platform and establishes the principles that guide its development.

---

# Vision Statement

Finance Platform aims to become a central hub for understanding personal wealth by providing users with a single place to organise, monitor and understand their long-term financial position.

---

# Project Philosophy

Finance Platform is founded on a simple principle:

> **Before people can start making money, they need to know where their money is.**

The project prioritises understanding before optimisation. Advanced analytical features will only be introduced once users can clearly understand what they own and how their wealth changes over time.

---

# Mission

To provide an intuitive platform that enables users to organise and understand their long-term wealth through a single application.

---

# Objectives

The Beta release aims to:

- Display total net worth.
- Organise assets and liabilities.
- Track historical changes.
- Present wealth using simple visualisations.
- Provide a stable foundation for future development.

---

# Long-Term Vision

Future releases may include:

- Live asset valuations.
- Platform integrations.
- Advanced analytics.
- Intelligent insights.
- Community contributions through open-source development.

These features will only be introduced after the core objective of understanding wealth has been achieved.

---

# Guiding Principles

Development will follow these principles:

- Simplicity before complexity.
- Understanding before optimisation.
- Accuracy before automation.
- User control over automation.
- Incremental development.

---

## Summary

### Key Points

- Finance Platform focuses on understanding long-term wealth.
- Beta establishes the foundation for future releases.
- Simplicity and clarity take priority over advanced features.
- Future development builds upon a stable core platform.
